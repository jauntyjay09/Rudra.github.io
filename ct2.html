STAYING STRONG

SHRI . D VIVEKANANDAN Shri D.Vivekanandan is a Former IAS Officer who was the first speaker of “A Chat to Change:Engaging Talks with Inspiring Individual” . He gave a motivational talk on staying strong during difficult situations by sharing various incidents of his life as an IAS officer and the life-changing lessons he learnt from those experiences.

BUSTING THE MYTHS AND TABOOS ABOUT AYURVEDA

SIMINAYANI Dr. Siminayani in our third live session, addressed all the myths and taboos and cleared them with logical and proven scientific facts.She expressed the fact that Ayurveda medicine is safe, nature-friendly,and that it is comprehensive of the body,the mind, and the spirit. THE ART OF ACTING

MR. REGINE ROSE The fourth live session was with Mr. Regine Rose who is an actor, acting trainee, and a theatre director. He also specializes in Stanislavsky system, Michael Chekov acting, Physical acting and Method acting. He explained in depth the various stages provided to an actor to perform his art. During the question and answer session, he related his acting experience to motivate young aspiring actors who attended the workshop.

COOKING THE GRANDMA'S RECIPE

CHEF SUNDAR With everyone bringing out their inner cook and trying different dishes during the period of self- isolation, RUDRA organized a cooking session by Chef Sundar for its fifth live session. The session started with a small description of the food which was going to be prepared by Chef Sundar. The food was prepared traditionally for what the chef liked to call as preparing food in grandma's style. The appetizing meal course looked mouth-watering delicious and the viewers were eager to try those recipes out.
