LH1 inaugration !
Event
  • The Open House of Open Community Rudra (NGO) was inaugurated by Prof. Susmitha Shyamsundar in the presence of other dignitaries, student coordinators and members on 20th February, 2020 in room number 926, LH from 1:00 pm to 2:00 pm. An inaugural address was given by the project lead.
  • Through her speech, the project lead explained the idea, the motto, and the working principles of the project RUDRA. She also stressed upon the importance of such a project and its impact on the society. All those who attended the event were made aware of the significance of voluntary donation and encouraged to do the same. The event became an initiative for donations by students which would be used for the aid of the people in need.
  • Prof. Susmitha Shyamsundar interacted with the student coordinators and members and shared her brainstorming ideas to enhance the scope of RUDRA. The event was concluded by a vote of thanks addressed by the project lead and a group photo.
