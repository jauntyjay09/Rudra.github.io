Field Visit
23 FEB   Chiguru Children’s Village, Amaravathi, Andra Pradesh  9:30 A.M. to 3:00 P.M.

The latest activity done for spreading positivity by the Open Community Rudra (NGO) is a field visit to the Chiguru Children’s Village. The students, along with the faculty coordinators, interacted with the children by conducting games sessions, motivational speeches, mock drills, and fun competitions that created an environment filled with laughter and joy. The students served the lunch which was sponsored by the club to the children. In the end, the donations and gifts were distributed. The gifts given to them became the reason for their teeth revealing smiles and love filled hugs. we express our deepest gratitude and appreciation to Mr. S Karthikeyan, Mr. Hari Kishan Kondaveeti, Dr. Prabha Selvaraj, Dr. D Sumathi, and Mr. Ramakrishna for joining us in our field visit. A special thanks to Arun to capture all the beautiful moments and help us make a difference.



LH Inauguration
20 FEB   926, LH, VIT-AP, Amaravathi  1:00 P.M. to 2:00 P.M.

The Open House of Open Community Rudra (NGO) was inaugurated by Prof. Susmitha Shyamsundar in the presence of other dignitaries, student coordinators and members. An inaugural address was given by the project lead. Through her speech, the project lead explained the idea, the motto, and the working principles of the project RUDRA. She also stressed upon the importance of such a project and its impact on the society. All those who attended the event were made aware of the significance of voluntary donation and encouraged to do the same. The event became an initiative for donations by students which would be used for the aid of the people in need. Prof. Susmitha Shyamsundar interacted with the student coordinators and members and shared her brainstorming ideas to enhance the scope of RUDRA. The event was concluded by a vote of thanks addressed by the project lead and a group photo.



Paint A Smile
15 FEB   Newton Hall, VIT-AP, Amaravathi, Andra Pradesh  4:00 P.M. to 6:00 P.M

RUDRA Open Community, in collaboration with the DIY club organized “Paint a Smile”, a pot painting competition. The competition was open to all the students of the university. The theme of the painting was dependent on the imagination of the participants who were supposed to bring out their creativity for the little ones, the children who are sheltered at orphanages. The pots painted by the participants were donated to them during the field trip conducted by RUDRA. The event witnessed an enormous number of participants who came out to be a part of this humble cause. All the participants gave the colourless pots a touch of warmth with their colours, paintbrushes and creative imagination. The judges evaluated all the pots and declared the winners. The event ended with a photo session and everyone feeling happy to be a part of such a noble cause. Special thanks to our judges: Dr. R. Thahiya Afzal, Mr. Hari Kishan Kondaveeti, and Mr. K Samuel Johnson. A note of thanks to Sanjeev for shooting the event and help us make this event a huge success.



Open House
15 FEB   303 MH-1, VIT-AP, Amaravathi, Andra Pradesh  4:00 P.M. to 6:00 P.M

The second Open House of Open Community Rudra (NGO) was conducted on 15th February, 2020 in room number 303, MH-1 from 4:00 pm to 6:00 pm. All the interested students for voluntary donations and gifts were informed to visit the Open House and donate their items. The collected donated items, pots painted by the participants of the “Paint a Smile” competition, and gifts were displayed in the Open House. The event witnessed many students who made donations. All the donations were registered simultaneously and each donation was appreciated by the coordinators. All the donations were segregated and packaged to be donated at the upcoming field trip.
