Paint a smile !
Event
  • RUDRA Open Community, in collaboration with the DIY club organized “Paint a Smile”, a pot painting competition on 15th February, 2020 in Newton Hall from 4:00 pm to 6:00 pm. The competition was open to all the students of the university.
  • The theme of the painting was dependent on the imagination of the participants who were supposed to bring out their creativity for the little ones, the children who are sheltered at orphanages. The pots painted by the participants were donated to them during the field trip conducted by RUDRA.
  • The event witnessed an enormous number of participants who came out to be a part of this humble cause. All the participants gave the colourless pots a touch of warmth with their colours, paintbrushes and creative imagination. The judges evaluated all the pots and declared the winners. The event ended with a photo session and everyone feeling happy to be a part of such a noble cause.

    • Special thanks to our judges: Dr. R. Thahiya Afzal, Mr. Hari Kishan Kondaveeti, and Mr. K Samuel Johnson. A note of thanks to Sanjeev for shooting the event and help us make this event a huge success.
