We at Rudra, believe in the principle that Love, indeed, Moves The World!

Our Vision :


To bring about a transformative change in the society, by combining the triad of ideas, viz. Voluntary Donation, Change in Ideation of Well Being & Artistic Recreation of Items,which otherwise are regarded as unusable. We understand, that, we make a living by what we get, but we make a life by what we give!
