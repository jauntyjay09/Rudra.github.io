MH1 inaugration !
Event
  • The Open House of Open Community Rudra (NGO) was inaugurated by Prof. Nandeesh Kumar and Prof.S. Karthikeyan in the presence of other dignitaries, student coordinators and members on 9th November, 2019 in room number 303, MH-1 from 1:00 pm to 2:00 pm. An inaugural address was given by the project lead.
  • Through his speech, the project lead explained the idea, the motto, and the working principles of the project RUDRA. He also stressed upon the importance of such a project and its impact on the society. All the donated items were put up for display as a part of the Open House.
  • A tour of the Open House was given to the faculty members and students. All those who attended the event were made aware of the significance of voluntary donation. The event became an initiative for donations by students which would be used for the aid of the people in need. The event was concluded by a vote of thanks addressed by the project lead and a group photo.
