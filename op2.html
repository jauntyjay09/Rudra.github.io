Open house 2.0 !
Event
  • The second Open House of Open Community Rudra (NGO) was conducted on 15th February, 2020 in room number 303, MH-1 from 4:00 pm to 6:00 pm. All the interested students for voluntary donations and gifts were informed to visit the Open House and donate their items.
  • The collected donated items, pots painted by the participants of the “Paint a Smile” competition, and gifts were displayed in the Open House.
  • The event witnessed many students who made donations. All the donations were registered simultaneously and each donation was appreciated by the coordinators.
  • All the donations were segregated and packaged to be donated at the upcoming field trip.
